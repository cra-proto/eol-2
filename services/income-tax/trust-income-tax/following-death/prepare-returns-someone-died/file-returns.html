T1 Income Tax and Benefit returns

Final return 70(1)

What return form to use: T1 Income Tax Package for the year the person died and for the province or territory the person lived at the time of death.

If an income tax package is not available for the year the person died, use the most recent year and write the year the you are filing for in the top right hand corner.

Optional Returns

Optional returns use the same return forms as the final return 70 (1), but marked to show which optional returns you want to file. You will need a separate form for each optional return you file.

Return for rights or things 70(2)

What return form to use: T1 Income Tax Package for the year the person died. Write "70(2)" in the top right corner of page 1 on the return.

Election to delay payment of income tax

In some cases, you can delay paying part of the amount owing from rights or things. However, the CRA still charges interest on any unpaid amount from the day after the due date to the date you pay the amount in full.

If you want to delay payment, you will have to give the CRA security for the amount owing. You also have to complete form T2075, Election to Defer Payment of Income Tax, under subsection 159(5) of the Income Tax Act by a Deceased Taxpayer's Legal Representative or Trustee. For more information, call 1-888-863-8657.

Return for a partner or proprietor 150(4)

What return form to use: T1 Income Tax Package for the year the person died. Write "150 (4)" in the top right corner of page 1 of the return.

Return for income from a graduated rate estate 104(23)(d)

What return form to use: T1 Income Tax Package for the year the person died. Write "104(23)(d)" in the top right corner on page 1 of the return.

T3 Trust Income Tax and Information Return

What form to use: Use form T3RET T3 Trust Income Tax and Information Return to file a T3 Trust Income Tax and Information return for the esate of someone who died.

Labelling the tax returns

Before you submit the returns after filling in the return, make sure in the identification and other information area of the return you:

  • write the type of return in the upper right corner of the first page
  • enter the identification information for the person who died in all the boxes
  • write "The Estate of the Late" before the name of the person who died
  • give your address as the mailing address
  • make sure the province or territory of residence on December 31 is the one where the person who died was living on the date of death
  • tick the box that applies to the marital status of the person who died at the time of death
  • enter the date of death on the proper line

Make sure you sign your name and indicate your title (for example, executor or administrator) in the area provided on the last page of the return.

How to submit the returns

Final return 70(1) and optional returns

If you are filing the returns yourself as the representative of someone who died, you will need mail them into your tax centre.

Online tax softwares do not currently support the submission of the final return 70(1) or optional returns.

EFLIE is only available to approved tax preparation service providers, such as accountants.

T3 Trust Income Tax and Information return

Online

You can submit documents online related to the T3 return through My Account or Represent a Client in the submit documents section.

By mail

You can also mail the T3 return to a trust processing tax centre..
