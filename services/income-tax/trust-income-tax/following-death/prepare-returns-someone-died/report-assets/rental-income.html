Rental income

On this page

  • Determine whether to report in the final return or T3
  • Calculate the deceased's rental income
  • Capital Cost Allowance recapture or terminal loss

Determine whether to report in the final return or T3

  • Before date of death: Option 1

    Rental income earned between January 1 and the date of death must be reported in the final personal income tax return 70(1) on lines 12599 and 12600.

  • After date of death: Option 2

    If the estate earned rental income after the date of death, it should be reported on line 8 of a T3 Trust Income and Information Return.

Calculate the deceased's rental income

Calculate the deceased's rental income using the accrual or cash methods. Refer to the T4036 Rental Income guide for further details.

The calculation is completed, for either the T1 or the T3, in a T776 Statement of Real Estate Rentals, which should be sent with the return.

Capital Cost Allowance recapture or terminal loss

If you ever claimed Capital Cost Allowance deductions on the rental property, you will need to calculate your recapture or terminal loss for the final return. Refer to recapture of capital allowance and terminal loss.
