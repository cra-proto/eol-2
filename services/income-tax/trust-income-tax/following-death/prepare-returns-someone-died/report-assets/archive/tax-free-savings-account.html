Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA)

In general, you do not need to report TFSA amounts as income on the final return.

The exception is amounts that appear in box 134 of your T4A slips. Can't find any references to what types of income this would be??

Increase in value after the date of death

The TFSA will cease to exist upon the date of death. This means that any increase in value of the investments or interest in the account will after the date of death will be taxable like other investments.

  • Report any capital gains from between the date of death and distribution to beneficiaries in a T3 Trust Income Tax return
