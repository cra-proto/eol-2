Prepare tax returns for someone who died

When someone dies, one or more T1 tax returns need to be filed to report their belongings, investments and other taxable income.

The representative may also be able to claim credits and deductions to reduce amounts owed by the person who died.

A T3 Trust Income Tax and Information Return might also need to be filed for the estate.
