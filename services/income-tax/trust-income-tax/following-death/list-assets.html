Prepare returns for someone who died

Make a list of assets and income

Select the types of assets and income owned by the deceased to create a list of assets for the estate and get specific instructions on how and where to report them.

You can also get a full list of the assets that need to be reported.

When you select a type of asset or income, more information about it may be available below the checklist.

Adjustment to pay

Adjustment to employment income when a collective agreement or other authorizing instrument has been signed after, the date of death, including retroactive:

  • salary, wages, and overtime from the end of the last pay period to the date of death
  • salary, wages and overtime for a pay period finished before the date of death, but paid after death
  • payment of vacation leave earned but not taken
