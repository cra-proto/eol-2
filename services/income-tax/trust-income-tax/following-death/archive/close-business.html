Close a business when someone dies - Sole-proprietor/partnership

  • Sole proprietor/partnership
  • Corporation

When a sole proprietor or a partner in a business disposes, the legal representative must ensure that any assets are transferred and their accounts closed.

On this page

  • Report business income in the final return
    • Input tax credits might need to be repaid
    • Farming or fishing equipment and property
  • Close the GST/HST account and transfer supplies or inventory
  • File both an income tax and GST/HST clearance certificate

Report business income in the final return

Income of a sole-proprietor or partner is reported as part of personal income taxes in the final personal income tax returns.
